Skender Pasha Mosque

Sarajevo

This mosque was chosen to be built as part of a larger urban-architectural project which encompasses the entire neighbourhood of Skenderija in central Sarajevo. The wheels are in motion, but the mosque is yet to be constructed. There was otherwise a mosque in the area which was demolished in the middle of the last century; the architect chose to design this version with a more modern aesthetic.
